  • Course Introduction

    We will go over course content, logistics, grading. Demonstrate our robot.

  • Gretchen Robot Assembly & Software Installation

    We will assemble 3D-printed parts, motors, sensors to create our robot. We will install our software development environment.

  • Introduction to Robotics & First Steps with Gretchen

    We will go over the basics of robotics. We will demonstrate various tasks using motor and camera control.

  • Tracking a Ball

    We will learn color spaces, traditional computer vision, and track an object with the robot's head.

  • Face Pose Estimation

    We will go over basics of machine learning and types of ML. We will use our robot to perform face detection and head pose estimation.

  • Introduction to Machine Learning & Object Detection with Deep Learning

    We will learn about basics of deep learning, perceptrons, and neural networks. We will train and detect objects with YOLO.

  • Project Ideas & Additional Libraries

    We will introduce project ideas to encourage you come up with your own ideas. We will show past projects. To help you with your projects, we will introduce you to additional libraries.

  • Project Presentations

    You will demonstrate and present your project.
